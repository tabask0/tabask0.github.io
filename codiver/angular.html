World of Angular

This is my character/items system with inventory.

It's an app where you can choose a desired character or item with different stats from a predefined list.

Each character/item is different by stats and rarity (the bigger the rarity, the most powerful it should be).

You can purchase characters or items by clicking on them and then adding them to the inventory by clicking the PLUS icon.
